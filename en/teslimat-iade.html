VoxoMix is a digital software subscription service. There is no physical product delivery. The purchased subscription plan is defined to your account immediately after payment is confirmed.

4. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time. After cancellation:

  • To cancel, you may write to info@voxomix.com

5. Refund Policy

Since VoxoMix is a digital service, under Article 49 of Turkey's Law No. 6502 on Consumer Protection and the Regulation on Distance Contracts, the right of withdrawal cannot be exercised for digital content once delivery has begun with the consumer's consent.

Nevertheless, a refund may be considered in the following cases:

  • The service could not be used at all due to a technical error
  • There was a problem with subscription activation
  • A duplicate (double) payment occurred

For refund requests, please contact info@voxomix.com within 7 days of the purchase date. Requests are evaluated within 5 business days at the latest.
