About

The story and mission behind VoxoMix

VoxoMix is a desktop application built to help musicians and music lovers better understand, learn and perform songs.

Using AI-powered stem separation technology, it splits any song into its vocal, drum, bass, guitar and other instrument layers. This lets musicians study, practice and learn every component of a track separately.

Our Mission

To offer an accessible tool that lets anyone understand and learn music in depth — without needing expensive studio equipment or software.

Technology

VoxoMix is built with Electron (desktop app framework), Python FastAPI (backend) and Demucs (Meta AI's open-source stem separation model). All processing happens on your own computer; your data is never sent to any server.
