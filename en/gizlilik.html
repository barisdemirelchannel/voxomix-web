Privacy Policy

How we process your personal data

Last updated: 19 June 2026

This English text is an unofficial translation provided for convenience. The Turkish version of this policy is the legally binding one; in case of any discrepancy, the Turkish version prevails.

1. Data Controller

This privacy policy explains how personal data is processed within the scope of the VoxoMix application and the www.voxomix.com website, operated by Barış Demirel, under Turkey's Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data (KVKK).

2. Data Collected

The following data may be collected while you use our service:

  • Account information: Email address, first and last name (during sign-up)
  • Payment information: Subscription plan and payment date (card details are processed by iyzico and are not stored by us)
  • Usage data: Number of songs uploaded, features used
  • Technical data: Operating system, app version

3. Purpose of Data Use

The data collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining the service
  • Subscription management and payment processing
  • Providing technical support
  • Improving the service
  • Fulfilling legal obligations
  • Where you have given explicit consent; sending commercial electronic messages by email, such as new-version notices, price updates, campaigns and new-product announcements (see Section 10)

4. Local Data Processing

The VoxoMix desktop application processes the music files you upload only on your local computer. Your audio files are never sent to or stored on any external server.

6. Data Security

Industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) and security measures are applied to protect your data. Your payment information is processed on iyzico's PCI-DSS compliant infrastructure.

7. Data Retention Period

Your account data is retained while your account is active and for 2 years after the account is deleted. Payment records are kept for 10 years as required by law.

8. Your Rights Under the KVKK

Under Article 11 of Law No. 6698 (KVKK), you have the following rights:

  • To learn whether your personal data is being processed
  • To request information if it has been processed
  • To request the deletion or destruction of your data
  • To request the correction of your data
  • To object to processing

To exercise these rights, you may contact info@voxomix.com.

9. Cookies

Our website uses only strictly necessary cookies for core functionality. No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are used.

10. Commercial Electronic Messages and Marketing

With the separate, explicit opt-in consent you provide during sign-up, we may send you commercial electronic messages by email, such as new-version notices, price updates, campaigns and new-product announcements. This consent is not a precondition for registering for the service; you can fully use the service even if you do not check it.

Commercial electronic messages are sent within the scope of Law No. 6563 on the Regulation of Electronic Commerce and its related regulation. Your consents are recorded in the Message Management System (İYS).

Opt-out: You may exercise your right to refuse free of charge at any time via the link at the bottom of every commercial email, by sending a request to info@voxomix.com, or through İYS. Your refusal request is processed within 3 business days at the latest.

Note: Informational emails relating to the operation of the service — such as subscription renewal, payment confirmation, email verification and password reset — are not commercial electronic messages and are therefore sent independently of this consent.
